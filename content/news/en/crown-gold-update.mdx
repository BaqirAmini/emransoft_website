## A Faster, More Professional Crown Gold

We are pleased to announce the latest update to Crown Gold, our premium dental clinic management system. This release focuses on two things clinics ask for most: faster patient communication and a cleaner, more professional workspace.

### Send a Patient's Invoice to WhatsApp in One Click

Billing is now effortless. Once an invoice is generated, a single click sends it straight to the patient's WhatsApp — no printing, no manual copying, no switching between apps. The patient receives a clear, professional invoice on the channel they already use every day.

### Send a Patient's Appointment to WhatsApp in One Click

Appointment details can now be shared the same way. With one click, the patient receives the date, time, and any instructions directly on WhatsApp. This keeps patients informed, reduces missed appointments, and saves your staff valuable time.

### A Refined, More Professional Dashboard

The Crown Gold dashboard has been redesigned with a cleaner layout, clearer typography, and better-organized information. Key numbers and daily activity are now easier to read at a glance, giving your clinic a more polished and professional feel for both staff and patients.

### Availability

These improvements are available now for all Crown Gold subscribers. Existing users can enable one-click WhatsApp sharing from the Settings panel; the refined dashboard is applied automatically. Clinics on the standard Crown plan can upgrade to Crown Gold to access these features.

To learn more or to upgrade, please contact our sales team or your account manager.
