## Major Update for Labra

We are excited to release version 2.0 of Labra, our dental laboratory management system. This update brings significant improvements based on feedback from our valued customers.

### What's New

#### Enhanced Order Management
The order management system has been completely redesigned for better efficiency. Lab technicians can now track orders through every stage of production with greater clarity.

#### Improved Production Tracking
Real-time production tracking allows laboratory managers to see exactly where each case stands, helping to meet deadlines and improve customer satisfaction.

#### Refined User Interface
We have updated the interface with a cleaner, more intuitive design that reduces training time and improves daily workflow.

#### Performance Improvements
Labra 2.0 is faster than ever, with optimized database queries and improved loading times.

### How to Upgrade

Existing customers can upgrade to Labra 2.0 by contacting our support team. The upgrade process is seamless and your existing data will be preserved.
