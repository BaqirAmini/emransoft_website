## Smarter Communication for Modern Clinics

We are excited to announce the latest update to Crown Gold, our premium dental clinic management system. This release introduces direct WhatsApp integration, making patient communication faster and more convenient than ever before.

### One-Click Invoice Sharing

With the new WhatsApp integration, clinics can now send invoices directly to patients through WhatsApp with a single click. Gone are the days of printing, emailing, or manually messaging each invoice. Simply generate the invoice in Crown Gold, click the WhatsApp button, and it is sent instantly to the patient's phone.

### Automated Appointment Reminders

Crown Gold can now automatically send appointment reminders and confirmations via WhatsApp. Patients receive their appointment details directly on their phone, reducing no-show rates and improving clinic efficiency. The entire process is seamless and requires no manual intervention once configured.

### Key Benefits

- **Time Savings** — Eliminate manual communication tasks and free up staff for higher-value work.
- **Improved Patient Experience** — Patients receive invoices and reminders on the channel they use most.
- **Higher Attendance Rates** — Automated WhatsApp reminders significantly reduce missed appointments.
- **Professional Image** — Modern, instant communication reinforces your clinic's professional brand.

### How It Works

The integration is built directly into the Crown Gold interface. After a simple one-time setup linking your clinic's WhatsApp Business account, the feature is available throughout the system:

1. **Invoicing Module** — Each generated invoice includes a WhatsApp send button.
2. **Appointment Module** — Reminders are sent automatically based on your configured schedule.
3. **Patient Records** — Patient phone numbers are automatically detected for WhatsApp delivery.

### Availability

The WhatsApp integration is available immediately for all Crown Gold subscribers. Existing users can activate the feature from the Settings panel. For clinics on the standard Crown plan, upgrading to Crown Gold is required to access this feature.
