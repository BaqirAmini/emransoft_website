## Welcome to Emransoft

Thank you for visiting Emransoft! Whether you are a potential customer, a partner, or simply curious about what we do, we are glad you are here.

### Who We Are

Emransoft is a software development company based in Kabul, Afghanistan, dedicated to building professional, reliable software solutions for healthcare providers and businesses.

Our team of passionate engineers works tirelessly to create products that make a real difference in the daily operations of clinics, laboratories, and pharmacies across Afghanistan.

### Our Products

We currently offer three main products:

1. **Crown** — Complete dental clinic management for patient records, appointments, billing, and reporting.
2. **Labra** — Dental laboratory management with order tracking, production management, and client communication.
3. **Tajviz** — Modern computerized prescription system replacing handwritten prescriptions.

### Our Commitment

We are committed to:

- **Quality** — Building software that meets international standards.
- **Reliability** — Creating solutions that work, even in challenging environments.
- **Support** — Providing responsive customer support to all our users.
- **Innovation** — Continuously improving our products with the latest technology.
