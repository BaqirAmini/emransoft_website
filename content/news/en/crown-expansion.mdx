## Growing Across Afghanistan

We are proud to announce that Crown, our comprehensive dental clinic management system, has now expanded its reach to over 10 provinces across Afghanistan. This milestone reflects the growing trust that dental professionals place in our software.

### Provinces Now Served

Crown is currently active in the following provinces:

- Kabul
- Herat
- Balkh (Mazar-i-Sharif)
- Kandahar
- Nangarhar (Jalalabad)
- Kunduz
- Takhar
- Logar
- Ghazni
- Daikundi

### Why Clinics Choose Crown

Crown has become the preferred choice for dental clinics in Afghanistan because of its unique features:

- **Offline Capability** — Works seamlessly even without internet connectivity.
- **Persian/Dari Interface** — Fully localized in Dari, Pashto, and English.
- **Comprehensive Reporting** — From patient records to financial reports, everything is at your fingertips.
- **Easy to Use** — Designed with the Afghan dental professional in mind.

### What's Next

We are actively working on expanding to even more provinces. If you are interested in bringing Crown to your clinic, please contact our team.
