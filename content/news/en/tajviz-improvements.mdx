## Better Prescriptions, Better Care

Tajviz, our computerized prescription system, has received a comprehensive update that brings new features and improvements to enhance the prescription experience for both doctors and patients.

### Key Improvements

#### Digital Prescription Templates
New customizable templates allow doctors to create prescriptions faster while maintaining accuracy and professionalism.

#### Medication Database
Tajviz now includes an expanded medication database with dosage guidelines, helping reduce prescription errors.

#### Patient History Integration
Doctors can now view a patient's complete prescription history, enabling better-informed treatment decisions.

#### Print and Share
Prescriptions can be printed in a clean, professional format or shared digitally with patients via messaging apps.

### Why Tajviz Matters

Handwritten prescriptions can be difficult to read and prone to errors. Tajviz eliminates these issues by providing clear, typed prescriptions that are easy for pharmacists and patients to understand.
